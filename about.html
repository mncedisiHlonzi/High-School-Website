About Us

SHENGEZA HIGH SCHOOL

Established in 1995 as a beacon of educational excellence, Shengeza High School stands proudly as a commercial school located in the picturesque Mnyayisa area, Ward 4, Umhlabuyalingana Local Municipality. Offering three streams - General, Science, and Commerce - the school has become synonymous with academic achievement and holistic development.

With a consistent annual learner enrollment of 700 - 750, Shengeza High School boasts a dedicated team of 25 teachers and support staff, including administrators, security personnel, and cleaners. The comprehensive curriculum includes a range of subjects, from Isizulu Home Language and English FAL to Mathematics, Physical Sciences, and Economics.

Under the visionary leadership of Mr. Ngubane SS as the Principal, Mr. Magcaba W as the Deputy Principal, and a dynamic team of Deputy Heads for Commerce, Science, and Communication & Languages, Shengeza High School operates with the utmost dedication to academic excellence.

Shengeza High School takes pride in its commendable pass percentages, reflecting the commitment to academic success: 76.7% in 2021, 80.1% in 2022, and an impressive 89.4% in 2023.

WORDS FROM OUR HEADMASTER

"I am Sydney Sboniso Ngubane, the third principal of Shengeza High School since its inception in 1995. I take immense pride in being an integral part of Shengeza High School, having joined in 2000 as a History and IsiZulu teacher at the age of 24. In 2013, at the age of 37, I was appointed as principal following the departure of Mr. MA Manyapye, who remains my mentor to this day.

Upon joining the school, my primary areas of focus were maintaining discipline for effective learning and teaching, restoring a culture of quality learning and teaching, protecting contact time, eliminating loitering during teaching hours, actively involving different stakeholders, fostering good human relations amongst staff, and fostering positive interactions with learners, parents, and the community at large. Additionally, I aimed to improve academic performance across all grades.

Through teamwork, I can confidently say that our learners are exhibiting exemplary behavior, as evidenced by their level of performance, punctuality, and adherence to the school uniform policy. As a result of various stakeholders' involvement, acts of vandalism and theft have decreased drastically.

Last year, in 2023, our matric class achieved an impressive 89.4%, a significant improvement from the previous year's 80.1%. This year, in 2024, our target is to attain a 100% pass rate with quality results."

~ Mr. SS Ngubane ~
