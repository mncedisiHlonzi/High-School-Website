STREAMS WE OFFER

Discover the array of streams we offer, each designed to provide a dynamic and enriching educational experience.

Science

The Science stream at Shengeza High School is tailored for students passionate about natural sciences, mathematics, and technology. With subjects like Physics, Chemistry, Life Science & Mathematics, students develop critical thinking skills, laying the foundation for careers in medicine, engineering, research, and technology-driven fields.

General

The General stream at Shengeza High School offers a well-rounded education with diverse subjects, including English, mathematical literacy, History, and electives. Tailored for students seeking a broad academic experience, this stream nurtures creativity, critical thinking, and effective communication skills.

Commerce

Shengeza High School's Commerce stream is ideal for students interested in business, economics, and finance. Featuring subjects like Accounting, Business Studies, Economics, and Mathematics, this stream equips students with essential knowledge for careers in finance, commerce, entrepreneurship, and related fields.
