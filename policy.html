Policy

POLICIES AND CONDUCT

Welcome to the Policy Page of Shengeza High School. Here, we uphold the values of transparency, accountability, and excellence in all aspects of our operations. Our school is well-led and organized, ensuring that our policies and codes of conduct are not just documents, but living principles that guide our community towards success.

Our Admission Policy ensures fairness and equity in the enrollment process, welcoming students from diverse backgrounds into our school family. With a clear Code of Conduct in place, we cultivate an environment of respect, responsibility, and safety, where students can thrive academically and socially. This contributes to a positive school culture characterized by well-behaved learners, smooth academic processes, and high performance by our students.

Our Assessment Policy ensures that evaluation methods are fair, transparent, and aligned with our educational objectives. This promotes a culture of continuous improvement and allows us to provide targeted support to students while celebrating their achievements.

At Shengeza High School, we are proud of the outcomes of a well-governed school – disciplined learners, engaged parents, dedicated educators, and outstanding academic performance. As you explore our official documents, we invite you to witness the commitment and dedication that define our school community.
