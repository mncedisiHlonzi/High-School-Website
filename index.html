STREAMS WE OFFER

Discover the array of streams we offer, each designed to provide a dynamic and enriching educational experience.

Science

The Science stream at Shengeza High School is tailored for students passionate about natural sciences, mathematics, and technology. With subjects like Physics, Chemistry, Life Science & Mathematics, students develop critical thinking skills, laying the foundation for careers in medicine, engineering, research, and technology-driven fields.

General

The General stream at Shengeza High School offers a well-rounded education with diverse subjects, including English, mathematical literacy, History, and electives. Tailored for students seeking a broad academic experience, this stream nurtures creativity, critical thinking, and effective communication skills.

Commerce

Shengeza High School's Commerce stream is ideal for students interested in business, economics, and finance. Featuring subjects like Accounting, Business Studies, Economics, and Mathematics, this stream equips students with essential knowledge for careers in finance, commerce, entrepreneurship, and related fields.

FAQS

What are the school's operating hours?

Shengeza High School operates from Monday to Thursday, 7:30 AM to 2:45 PM, and on Fridays from 7:30 AM to 1:30 PM.

What streams does Shengeza High School offer?

Shengeza High School offers three streams: General, Science, and Commerce.

How many teachers and support staff are there at the school?

Shengeza High School is supported by a dedicated team of 25 teachers, administrators, security personnel, and cleaners.

What subjects are offered at Shengeza High School?

The school offers a comprehensive range of subjects, including IsiZulu Home Language, English FAL, Life Orientation, Life Sciences, Agricultural Sciences, Mathematics, Mathematical Literacy, Physical Sciences, History, Tourism, Economics, Business Studies, and Accounting.

Who are the school's current leaders?

The school is led by Mr. Ngubane SS as the Principal, Mr. Magcaba W as the Deputy Principal, and various Deputy Heads overseeing specific streams and departments.

How can I contact the school administration?

You can contact the school administration through the provided contact details on the website or by visiting the school in person during office hours.
